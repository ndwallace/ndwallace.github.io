The Challenge

Modern agriculture increasingly relies on data-driven decision making, but there's a gap between raw sensor data and actionable knowledge. Farmers need to understand not just what's happening in their fields right now, but how environmental conditions are changing over time and how those changes affect their operations.

Application: Pasture Forage Monitoring

We applied the Eco-CPS framework to the problem of measuring and modelling pasture forage availability for grazing livestock. This is a critical challenge for farmersâ€”knowing how much feed is available across their property determines stocking decisions, paddock rotations, and supplementary feeding requirements.

The system combines:

  • Robotic sensing of pasture biomass and composition
  • Spatial modelling using Gaussian Processes
  • Temporal predictions based on weather and growth models
  • Integration with livestock tracking data
